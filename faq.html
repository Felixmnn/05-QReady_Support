Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Here you can find answers to the most common questions about Q-Ready.

Why can’t I edit questions from modules I copied?

You can only edit questions from modules you created yourself. Questions from copied modules are locked to preserve the integrity of the original module.

Why does my answer status not change?

Make sure your internet connection is stable when submitting answers. If the issue persists, restart the app.

What is LaTeX?

LaTeX is a typesetting system used for technical and scientific content. In Q-Ready, you can use it to format mathematical expressions and equations.

How long does it take for my energy to recover?

Energy recovers at a rate of 1 point every 2 hours, up to a maximum of 10 points. You can get extra energy by watching ads or buying energy packs.

Can I change my selected learning goals?

Yes, you can change your learning goals at any time in your profile under Profile - Edit Education Goals.

Why is my LaTeX not working?

Use KaTeX syntax as documented at https://katex.org/docs/supported.html. Make sure delimiters for inline ($...$) and display math (\[...\]) are correct.
